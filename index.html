About Us

As a chapter of the official Unicef Organization, we dedicate ourselves to assisting children worldwide through fundraisers and donations.

As a club of Folsom High, we help directly in our community by providing a strong volunteer team.

  • Every child has the right to survive and thrive
  • Every child has the right to learn
  • Innovating to drive results for every child
